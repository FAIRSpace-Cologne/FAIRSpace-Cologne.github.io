Data Stewardship Network Meetup

April 5, 2025 Networking Event FAIRspace Cologne Office

About the Meetup

Monthly networking event for data stewards across institutions. Share experiences, discuss challenges, and collaborate on solutions for common data management issues. This informal gathering provides an excellent opportunity to build connections within the data stewardship community.

The Data Stewardship Network serves as a platform for data professionals to exchange best practices, discuss emerging trends, and foster collaboration across institutions in the Cologne region and beyond.

Event Format

This is an informal, conversational meetup designed to encourage open dialogue and networking among data stewards. The evening will feature:

  • Welcome and Networking: Arrive early to meet fellow data stewards over refreshments
  • Featured Presentation: Short talk on a timely data management topic
  • Lightning Talks: 10-minute presentations from attendees on their projects
  • Open Discussion: Group discussion on challenges and solutions
  • Informal Networking: Continue conversations in smaller groups

Featured Topic for April 2025

"Implementing Research Data Management Policies in Your Institution"

Our keynote speaker, Dr. Anna Hartmann from the University of Bonn, will discuss the strategic considerations and practical steps for implementing institutional research data management policies. This presentation will cover:

  • Policy development process and stakeholder engagement
  • Common challenges and how to overcome them
  • Integration with existing infrastructure and workflows
  • Measuring compliance and success
  • Real-world case studies from German institutions

Discussion Topics

Attendees are encouraged to suggest topics for discussion. Previous meetup highlights have included:

  • Challenges in implementing FAIR principles across diverse departments
  • Data steward roles, responsibilities, and career development
  • Metadata management at scale
  • Balancing open data with data protection requirements
  • Building sustainable data management infrastructure
  • Training and capacity building for researchers
  • Funding data management activities
  • International collaboration and interoperability

Who Should Attend

This meetup is for anyone involved in research data management, including:

  • Data stewards and data managers
  • Research librarians and information specialists
  • IT professionals supporting research
  • Research office staff
  • Researchers interested in data management
  • Faculty and administrators
  • Anyone working to advance research data management practices
