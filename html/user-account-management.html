User Account Management

This guide provides step-by-step instructions on how to create, manage, and administer user accounts in FAIRdata Cologne. Follow these procedures to set up and maintain user accounts for your organization.

Creating a User Account

Step 1: Access the Registration Page

Navigate to the FAIRdata Cologne login page. Click on the "Create New Account" or "Sign Up" button located below the login form.

Step 2: Fill in Personal Information

Complete the registration form with the following information:

  • First Name: Your given name
  • Last Name: Your family name
  • Email Address: A valid email address for account verification
  • Institution: Select your organization from the dropdown list
  • Department: Your department or research group

Step 3: Create Login Credentials

Choose a username and password:

  • Username: 3-20 characters, alphanumeric with underscores
  • Password: At least 12 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password for verification

Step 4: Accept Terms and Conditions

Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Check the checkbox to confirm you have read and understood these documents.

Step 5: Verify Your Email

A verification email will be sent to the address you provided. Click the verification link in the email within 24 hours to activate your account.

Logging Into Your Account

Step 1: Navigate to Login Page

Go to the FAIRdata Cologne homepage and click on "Login" in the top navigation bar.

Step 2: Enter Credentials

Enter your username (or email) and password in the login form.

Step 3: Enable Two-Factor Authentication (Optional)

If two-factor authentication is enabled on your account, enter the verification code from your authentication app or check your email for a code.

Step 4: Access Your Dashboard

Once logged in successfully, you will be directed to your user dashboard where you can manage your profile and access platform features.

Managing Your Profile

Editing Profile Information

To update your profile:

  1. Click on your username in the top-right corner of the navigation bar
  2. Select "Profile Settings" from the dropdown menu
  3. Edit your personal information as needed
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your profile

Changing Your Password

To change your password:

  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Click on the "Security" tab
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password (must meet security requirements)
  5. Confirm your new password
  6. Click "Update Password"

Managing Notifications

To customize your notification preferences:

  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Click on the "Notifications" tab
  3. Toggle notification types on or off as desired
  4. Select your preferred notification delivery method (email, in-app, or both)
  5. Save your preferences

User Roles and Permissions

Role Descriptions

  • Visitor: Browse and search published metadata. No login required.
  • Contributor: Create and edit metadata records. Can submit records for review but cannot publish.
  • Publisher: Review and approve metadata records. Can publish records and manage published content.
  • Administrator: Full access to all platform features including user management, system settings, and configuration.

Requesting Role Changes

If you need a different role or permission level:

  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Click "Request Role Change"
  3. Select the desired role
  4. Provide justification for the role change
  5. Submit your request

Your request will be reviewed by administrators and you will receive notification of approval or denial.

Account Security

Best Practices for Account Security

  • Use a strong, unique password with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  • Enable two-factor authentication for additional security
  • Never share your credentials with others
  • Log out when you're done, especially on shared computers
  • Regularly review your login activity in the Security tab
  • Update your password at least every 90 days

If You Suspect Unauthorized Access

If you suspect your account has been compromised:

  1. Immediately change your password
  2. Review your recent activity in the Security tab
  3. Contact support at support@fairspace-cologne.de
  4. Consider enabling two-factor authentication

Deactivating Your Account

Account Deactivation Process

To deactivate your account:

  1. Go to Profile Settings
  2. Scroll to the bottom and click "Deactivate Account"
  3. Confirm that you understand the implications
  4. Enter your password for verification
  5. Your account will be deactivated immediately

Note: Deactivated accounts can be reactivated within 30 days. After 30 days, the account and all associated data will be permanently deleted.
