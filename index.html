About Us

FAIRspace Cologne is an initiative focused on supporting research data management and promoting the FAIR principles - Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. Our mission is to provide tools, training, and infrastructure that empower researchers to manage, share, and reuse data effectively.

We collaborate with universities, research institutions, and data stewards to ensure scientific data is handled transparently and sustainably. Our goal is to make open science not just possible but practical.
